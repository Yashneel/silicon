Vaccination schedule for a child born: January 1, 2021

This is the vaccination schedule for a child born on January 1, 2021 who is up-to-date on their immunizations.

If your child has missed one or more vaccines, contact your health unit or doctor's office as soon as possible to make an appointment for your child to get caught up.

Publicly funded (free) vaccines are available through your health unit (also called public health units, community health centers and primary care homes) and doctors' offices. Services vary across B.C.
